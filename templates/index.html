Welcome

“The beautiful thing about learning is nobody can take it away from you.”

Kia Ora and welcome to Guitar Group. This is the place you want to be if you are a lover music and learning, like us. We are here to help you develop your own taste and identity, and grow in what you love the most.

Music learning is a valuable skill in your life so make it count by learning with us!

About Us

"Every guitarist has something unique about their playing. They just have to identify and develop it."

Based in Samuel Marsden Collegiate School, Guitar Group is a music club that is open for all students. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced player, you can find a place for yourself amongst our lovely group.

Over the course of the year, we help you develop your skills so that you can perform at events such as showcase and cultural awards.
