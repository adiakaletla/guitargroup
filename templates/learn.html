Want to learn the basics?

“One step at a time is all it takes to get you there.”

There is no greater experience than learning to play an instrument, regardless of age. Unfortunately, even though many people try to learn the guitar, most beginners quit after just a few months.

At guitar group, we take care to adapt to the student and cover every topic they would like to learn about, even the very fundamentals like the guitar's anatomy. Before attending guitar group, check out the fundamentals listed below to get a general sense of the guitar, and then feel free to practise them at home.

Anatomy of the guitar

The guitar has various parts to it and having a good understanding of the anatomy of the guitar will enable you to get the most out of your practises. In the slideshow, we can see the more important parts of thr guitar which you should know.
They are: the tuning pegs, fret board, strings, sound hole, the bridge, and the guitar body.
